Peru

Peru is a very touristy country due to its beautiful landscapes, rich history, and delicious food. From the ancient ruins of Machu Picchu to the vibrant city life in Lima, there is something for everyone to enjoy. The diverse geography offers a range of activities, including hiking in the Andes, exploring the Amazon rainforest, and relaxing on the beaches of the Pacific coast. Peruvian cuisine is also renowned worldwide, with dishes like ceviche, lomo saltado, and pisco sour delighting visitors.
